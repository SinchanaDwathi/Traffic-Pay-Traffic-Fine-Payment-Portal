About the Portal

Digitising Road Safety Compliance

TrafficPay is an initiative by the Ministry of Road Transport & Highways to streamline traffic challan payments across India — secure, real-time, and fully digital.

🔒

Bank-Grade Security

256-bit SSL encryption, 2FA authentication, and RBI-regulated payment processing ensure your transactions are always protected.

⚡

Instant Processing

Payments reflect in real-time. No delays, no pending queues — your challan status updates the moment your payment clears.

📋

Legally Valid Receipts

Every transaction generates a court-admissible digital receipt with a unique transaction ID, timestamped and verifiable.

🌐

All India Coverage

Integrated with traffic enforcement databases across 28 states and 8 union territories for complete national coverage.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Optimized for all devices — smartphone, tablet, or desktop. Pay your challan from anywhere, anytime.

♻️

Paperless & Green

Zero paper, zero visits to traffic offices. Do your part for the environment while staying compliant with traffic laws.
